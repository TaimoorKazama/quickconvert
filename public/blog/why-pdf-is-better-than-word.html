Why PDF is Better Than Word

When it comes to sharing or printing documents, PDFs have become the universal standard. But why exactly is PDF better than Word?

1. Consistent Formatting

Word files often change layout or font style on different computers. PDFs lock your design and keep it consistent everywhere.

2. Security and Privacy

PDFs allow you to add passwords, watermarks, and restricted access — something Word files can’t do as effectively.

3. Universal Compatibility

Almost every device — phone, tablet, or computer — can open PDFs without needing special software.

4. Easy to Share and Print

Because of their fixed layout, PDFs print perfectly every time and are lightweight for emailing.
