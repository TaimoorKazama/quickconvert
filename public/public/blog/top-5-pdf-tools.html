Top 5 PDF Tools You Should Know

PDF tools make working with documents simple. Here are the top 5 free tools that help you create, convert, and manage PDFs easily.

1. QuickConvert

A free online tool to convert Word (.docx) files into PDF in seconds. No ads, no signups — just quick, clean conversion.

2. SmallPDF

Offers a variety of tools to merge, compress, and convert PDFs. Great for students and professionals alike.

3. iLovePDF

Another popular option for editing and converting PDF files, with mobile and web apps available.

4. PDFCandy

A clean interface for editing, converting, and splitting PDFs — completely free and browser-based.

5. Adobe Acrobat Online

The most advanced tool for PDF management, though it may require a subscription for premium features.
